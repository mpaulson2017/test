Prairie Course: Tee to Green

Our Prairie Course provides players with a little easier of a round compared to the Ocean Course. It is known for its great course layout, with varying holes tee to green. Also for its old-style links look, giving players a sentimental feel.

This course provides one of the most enjoyable rounds that you can find in the area. With a great tee-to-green layout the Prairie Course will no doubt get you to come back.
