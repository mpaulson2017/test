About Us

Our course was designed by golf course architect Pete Dye. Some courses he designed are:

  • Harbour Town
  • Ocean Course at Kiawah Island Resort
  • River Course at Blackwolf Run.

Here at Wildcat Links we seek out to provide the best experience for our members. Our months of operation are April through October and for months November-March openings depend on weather. Courses are free to play Thanksgiving week and Christmas week!(As long as there is no snow)

We also offer lessons here at the club. Either by appointment with our pro Matt, or sign up for one of our weekend sessions!

  1. Friday 5:00 pm - 6:00 pm
  2. Saturday 11:00 am - 12:00 pm
  3. Sunday 1:00 pm - 2:00 pm
